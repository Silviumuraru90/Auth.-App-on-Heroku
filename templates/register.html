Register


Note:

- Username's uppercase chars will be automatically converted to lowercase;
- The username should have between 5 to 10 chars;
- Trailing spaces are not allowed. Spaces are permitted only within the string;
- The password should have at least 10 chars;
- The password should contain uppercase chars as well;
- The password should contain lowercase chars as well;
- The password should contain at least one number;
- The password should contain either of the following special symbols: [@_!#$%^&*()<>?/\|}{~:;,.]
- Password should not contain parts of the username.
